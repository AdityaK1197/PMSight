What's inside

Everything a PMS firm needs
to understand its own business

10 modules. All your data. One dashboard that works from your own Google Sheet — no database, no IT team, no complexity.

📊
AUM Intelligence
Track AUM trends across all 5 financial years. Quarter-wise, FY-wise, channel-wise. See exactly where your assets are growing and where they're at risk.
💰
Revenue & P&L
Management fees, performance fees, exit fees — all tracked separately. Net P&L vs cost vs revenue on one screen. Know your profitability at a glance.
🎯
Channel Analytics
Direct, Distributor, and PL Mgmt channels compared side-by-side. Revenue, AUM, and net earnings by channel. Know which channel is truly profitable.
👥
RM Performance KRA
Track each Relationship Manager's AUM targets, actual achievement, client meetings, and NPS scores. Quarterly scorecards with ratings.
💼
Business Viability
Break-even AUM, cost coverage ratio, revenue-to-cost multiple. Adjust your cost baseline and see scenarios instantly. Know exactly how viable your business is.
🔒
Data Stays With You
Your data lives in your own Google Sheet. We never store it. Dashboard reads directly from your Sheet — no data leaves your control. SEBI-friendly architecture.
